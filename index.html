About us

We are a co-ed club team open to all University of Maryland, College Park students and faculty with any skill-level. We host three practices a week that allow you to meet and hit with other club tennis players in a fun and casual environment.
Those who wish to compete may challenge their way up our ladder system. Players can also compete in home/away dual matches and tournaments based on ladder position, attendance, and predicted difficulty of match.

Practices

Mon 8:15-10:15pm
Wed 8:15-10:15pm
Thurs 8:15-10:15pm
    The structure of practice depends on the amount of individuals in attendance. For the first 10-15 minutes, members are encouraged to hop on the courts and warm up. Officers will designate courts for hitting, games, drills, and in certain cases, match play.
    Hitting courts may be split into singles or doubles, with players rotating out after 3 points. Games include Around the World, King of the Court, etc. and we are open to suggestions! Drills are for improving certain shots, and officers will feed balls and give feedback if requested.
    Wednesday practices are reserved for beginners unless otherwise stated to ensure their understanding of the game and basic strokes.
    Attendance is not mandatory, and you may come and go depending on your schedule.
    Practice will be cancelled in cases of inclement weather, so look out for an email or post in our Facebook group.
